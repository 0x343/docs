Introduction


HKBetaTestnet is a blockchain-based application (DApp) on the Sepolia Base network, a testbed for Ethereum. With this DApp, you can:


  • Daily check-in: Earn 1 Point every day with just a click of a button.

  • Betting: Place a bet on your chosen number to win up to 10 Points or more.

  • Referral program: Invite friends and earn 5 Points when they place a bet.

  • Points: The tokens used in the DApp cannot be transferred directly to another person.

This tutorial will walk you through the steps to get started with the DApp, from setting up your wallet to interacting with all the features.

You only need a web browser (like Chrome) and the MetaMask crypto wallet extension, and other wallets that support EVM for PC mode, and for mobile mode you just need to install the MetaMask wallet app and other wallets that support EVM.

Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:


    Device with Browser:

    Computer or mobile with browser like Google Chrome, Firefox, or Safari for PC mode. Or prepare MetaMask application and other wallet that supports EVM which can be downloaded in Play Store and App Store (iOS).

    MetaMask Installed:

    Computer: Add MetaMask extension from metamask.io. Or another wallet that supports EVM


    Mobile: Install the MetaMask app, or another wallet that supports EVM from the App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android).


    Get Sepolia ETH:

    A little ETH on the Sepolia network for transaction fees get it for free from faucer provider.

    Access to the DApp:

    Go to the Lotto Chain Beta Testnet DApp.
