Step 6: Checking Your Balance and History


  1. Check Your Points Balance:
    Navigate to the Balance: > Point: section to view your total accumulated Points.



  2. Faucet Check-In History:
    Visit Your Faucet History to see your daily check-in records.



  3. Betting History:
    Go to Your Ticket History for detailed information about your placed bets.



  4. Referee List:
    Check My Invited Friends List to view the friends you’ve referred.



  5. Error Handling:
    If your data is not loading, try refreshing the page or disconnecting and reconnecting your wallet.
