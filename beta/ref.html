Step 3: Using the Referral Program


Invite friends and earn 5 Points when they place a bet.

  1. Referral Link Detection:
    • When a user opens the DApp using a referral link (e.g., ?uid=invitersid123), the system will automatically detect and process the referral.
    • If the user has not been referred before, a transaction will be triggered to link the referral ID. This requires confirmation in MetaMask or another EVM-compatible wallet.


  2. Automatic Referral ID Setup:
    • After connecting the wallet, if you haven’t set your own referral ID yet, the system will automatically generate and assign a unique one for your account.
    • This process runs silently in the background and requires one-time confirmation.


  3. Share Your Referral ID:
    • Once your referral ID has been set, you can find and copy it from the Referral section in the DApp.
    • Share your referral link (e.g., https://lottochain.xyz/beta/?uid=yourUid123) with others.


  4. Friends Join and Confirm:
    • When your friends open the link, connect their wallet, and haven't been referred yet, your ID will be set as their referrer automatically.
    • They will be prompted to confirm the transaction to complete the referral binding.


  5. Earn Rewards: You will automatically receive 5 Points once your referred friend successfully places a bet.

  6. Error Handling:
    • “You have already been referred”: The user cannot be referred again after the first successful referral binding.
    • “Referral ID not valid”: Make sure the referral link includes a valid UID parameter.
  7. Tip: Invite more friends using your referral link to increase your Point earnings automatically.
